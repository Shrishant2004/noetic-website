About Noetic

Noetic is an AI keyboard for iPhone that puts powerful writing tools exactly where you need them — inside the keyboard itself. No copy-pasting between apps. No context switching. Just type, tap, and write better.

We built Noetic because we got tired of switching apps just to fix a badly worded message. So we built one that actually does — for every text field, in every app, for every kind of writing.

Our mission is simple: make clear, confident writing accessible to everyone. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who wants to sound better in a text message — Noetic is for you.
