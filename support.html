How can we help?

Most questions answered below. If not, contact us directly.

Step 1: Open Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards
Step 2: Tap "Add New Keyboard"
Step 3: Select Noetic from the list
Step 4: Go back to Keyboards → tap Noetic → enable "Allow Full Access"

After this, you can switch to Noetic by tapping the globe icon on your keyboard.
Full Access is an iOS requirement for any keyboard that needs internet access. Noetic needs it to send text to our AI for processing. Without it, the AI features cannot work. Full Access does NOT mean we can read everything you type — we only process text when you tap a button.
Each AI action (grammar fix, tone rewrite, etc.) uses one credit. Free users receive a daily allowance that resets at midnight. You can earn additional credits by watching a short rewarded video ad inside the app. Pro and Premium subscribers receive higher daily limits or unlimited credits.
Go to Settings in the Noetic app → tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your subscription and Pro or Premium status should be restored immediately.
